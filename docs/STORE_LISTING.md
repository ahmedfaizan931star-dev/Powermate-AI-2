# PowerMate AI — Play Store Listing Draft

## Short description

Test charging speed, compare chargers, protect battery health, and use a clean AOD-style charging display.

## Full description

PowerMate AI is a premium offline-first battery and charging control center for Android.

Use it to view real charging speed, estimated wattage, voltage, temperature, charging status, battery health, and charging history. Run a charger diagnostic test to get a charger score, cable score, and stability score. Enable smart alerts for 80%, 90%, full charge, slow charging, charger disconnect, and overheating.

PowerMate AI also includes a beautiful AOD-style charging display with AMOLED-friendly design, night dim mode, burn-in protection, and multiple included styles.

Core features:

- Live ampere and wattage monitor
- Fast and slow charging detection
- Voltage and temperature readings
- Charger quality score
- Cable quality score
- Charging stability score
- Charging history and summaries
- Battery care reminders
- AOD-style charging display
- Battery widgets
- Offline-first privacy
- No account required

Note: Some phones do not expose accurate current sensor data. On unsupported devices, PowerMate AI shows safe fallback states instead of fake readings.
