# PowerMate AI Competitive Deep Research

## Main competitor: AmpereFlow
Public sources describe AmpereFlow as a battery speed/AOD app with real-time ampere and wattage, precise battery percentage, fast charging detection, battery health, voltage, temperature, battery alerts, customizable speedometer, AOD display and charging animations. Third-party pages also describe AOD widgets/customization, charge/discharge rate, power consumption, max capacity and health analysis.

## Other major competitor feature buckets

### AccuBattery
- Battery health and actual capacity in mAh
- Charge alarm to unplug at safer levels
- Battery wear per charge session
- Battery usage and discharge tracking
- Historical charge/discharge cycle analysis

### Ampere
- Charging/discharging current comparison
- Charger/cable testing
- Pro widgets, notifications and alerts
- Clear warning that readings depend on charger, cable, device, brightness, Wi-Fi and GPS

### Battery Guru
- Real-time battery health, capacity, current, voltage, watts and temperature
- Health degradation over time
- Charging performance comparison for chargers/cables
- Widgets and customizable notifications/alarms

### Charge Meter
- Fastest charger/cable detection
- Remaining charge time and use time
- Real battery capacity estimate
- Discharge speed and app consumption
- Premium: dark mode, PiP, widgets, no ads

## Important honesty

No Android app should fake battery precision. If a phone does not expose accurate current, charge counter or fuel-gauge properties, PowerMate AI should clearly show unsupported/low-confidence states instead of inventing numbers.
